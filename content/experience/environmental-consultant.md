**Project**: Strengthening Policy-level and Grassroot Capacity to Enhance Climate Change Adaptation for Marginalized Communities

**Duties and Responsibilities**:

* Supported the design and implementation of community-based initiatives that strengthened civic participation and local ownership in climate adaptation planning.
* Conducted participatory civic engagement assessments with marginalized communities and youth groups, applying a practical GEDSI lens (who participates, whose voice is missing, barriers to access) to capture priorities and translate them into policy-relevant outputs
* Designed and delivered capacity-building sessions for 90+ youth leaders across six municipalities on advocacy, safe community outreach, and constructive engagement with local decision-makers
* Facilitated policy dialogue consultations with municipal and district officials to strengthen responsiveness of local plans to community-identified priorities and strengthen trust between citizens and institutions
* Coordinated between local governments, CSOs, and community representatives to ensure inclusive participation, manage expectations, and reduce governance gaps during implementation
