**Duties and Responsibilities**:

* Coordinated and led multi-municipality programme implementation, overseeing field staff, enumerators, and short-term consultants while strengthening citizen and youth engagement in local service delivery through structured outreach, grievance mechanisms, and collaboration with municipal counterparts
* Designed and implemented RBM/M&E systems (planning tools, indicators, data collection workflows) to track outputs, outcomes, and learning; contributed to evidence-based reporting for donors and government partners
* Worked with municipal governments, youth groups, waste workers, and civil society partners to operationalize inclusive participation, paying attention to GEDSI considerations in community consultations and training participation
* Advocated for policies that foster circular economy principles and sustainable practices into municipal operations, fostering systematic resource efficiency and reducing environmental impact
* Supported development and rollout of digital tools (web dashboard + mobile application) that enabled citizens, including young people, to report issues and support civic participation in local accountability processes
* Led capacity-building for enumerators, municipal staff, and community volunteers on survey ethics, community engagement, and use of digital platforms for citizen reporting

**Selected Programme Achievements**:

* Coordinated household-level surveys covering ~70,000 households across six municipalities, strengthening local evidence for planning and decision-making.
* Supported training of 500+ municipal staff, waste workers, and community members (including youth participants) on participatory approaches, service delivery feedback loops, and data use.
* Delivered outreach and awareness campaigns reaching 30,000+ individuals, promoting public participation, accountability, and environmental rights
