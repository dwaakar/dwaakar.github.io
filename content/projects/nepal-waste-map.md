**About Nepal Waste Map**  

Nepal Waste Map is digital waste management and data collection system which includes a comprehensive web-based dashboard and mobile application. The technology platform allows cities and municipalities to undertake powerful analysis of waste-related data; provide waste collection and management information; and enable citizens to report waste dumping, burning, and irregular waste collection services. 

**Functional and Operational Component**:

Nepal Waste Map platform (both project and digital ecosystem) is based on the 5 functional and operational components. The components include:

**Waste in Neighbourhood** – Subcomponents include: Waste dumping, waste burning, collection issues and customer service feedback

**Waste Infrastructure** – Subcomponents include Waste bins, transfer stations, scrap dealers, waste processing centers and landfill site

**Waste Collection** - Subcomponents include Collection companies, collection routes, collection schedules, collection points, frequency

**Building Category** – Subcomponents include Residential, commercial, Institutions, hospitals, industries, farmhouse, no. of floors

**Sustainable Waste Management** - Subcomponent include Joined collection service or not, buildings that segregate waste, building that compost waste, buildings that recycle waste.

The process of implementing Nepal Waste Map:

**Mapping the Stakeholders**: Stakeholders working on waste management other than Local Government

**Data Collection**: Mapping the entire data related to waste management. 

**Technological Enhancements**: System upgrade and maintenance catered to local/provincial government

**Capacity Development**: Familiarization and Hands-on training on how to use NWM platform to local level and waste management companies

**Follow up and Community trainings**: Trainings related to the issues related to waste management.
