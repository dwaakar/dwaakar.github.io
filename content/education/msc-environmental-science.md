I completed a Master of Science in Environmental Science from the Central Department of Environmental Science at Tribhuvan University. The program offered a strong blend of theoretical depth and applied environmental training, emphasizing ecosystem processes, climate-related sciences, and advanced analytical tools. Across four semesters, I studied subjects spanning ecology, atmospheric and earth sciences, environmental quality management, climate resilience, environmental economics, geospatial analysis, hydrology, modelling, and interdisciplinary environmental studies.

Alongside coursework, I engaged extensively in practical components, including laboratory work, field research, community-based projects, case studies, and technical skill development in remote sensing and GIS. The degree culminated in a research dissertation, which allowed me to conduct independent, focused investigation within my specialization areas—Freshwater Environment, Water Resource Management, and Restoration Ecology.
