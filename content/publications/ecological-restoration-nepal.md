abstract: This chapter presents a comprehensive description of Nepal’s
  conservation and restoration strategies and policies. The goal of the policies
  is to foster sustainable development and safeguard the unique and diverse
  ecosystems. The escalating population has increased the demand for natural
  resources like forestry products, water, land and minerals, which could lead
  to a degradation of ecosystem services. Some restoration projects are
  improving the ecosystem functions and services, but intensive efforts are
  still needed. By adopting ecosystem restoration and resource conservation
  strategies, Nepal improves ecosystem functions and services, and livelihoods.
  An integrated approach to restoration offers additional benefits, creating
  employment opportunities in the country’s post-pandemic economic recovery.
